Lakes We Offer

Fisherman Stories invites you to explore some of the best lakes in Sweden

Lake Mjörn

Welcome to lake Mjörn! Lake with rich zander stock and perfect location. Mjörn is definitely the best lake in Gothenburg surroundings. The lake is a small inland sea with averige depth of 20m. Mjörn is a stream lake,with many islands that gives the impression of being an archipelago. One of the islands, Risön, is a nature reserve.Mjörn with its varied landscape provides a nice natural experience and enjoyment for both body and soul.

Lake Fegen

Lake Fegen is located right in the midel where the regions of Halland, Småland and Västergötland meet. Lake Fegen,offers shallow bays, great depths, narrow bays, large open "floars", many islands, smooth rock outcrops and wonderful sandy beaches with no less than 17 different species of fish. Lake is unique in the world for the spring-spawning grayling,interesting birdlife.This is the lake with the good water quality which is now become a nature reserve and home to many wild animals.

Lake Ralången

One of Småland's best zander waters, Ralången is known for good zander fishing and especially for record-breaking individuals. Pike fishing in Ralången is also good. Angling competitions are organized in the lake where very large catches of especially roach and bream are usually noted. Ralången is part of the Svartån catchment area and is located 4 kilometers north of Aneby.
